After taking the charge of the facility in 2005, I initiated basic research using adult and embryonic stem cells.

About Us

Get to know us

Established in 2005, Stem Cell Facility has been a pioneer in the field of Stem cell research. The facility’s main motive is to decipher/explore the potential of stem cells in basic and translational research, thereby, accelerating/pushing forward stem cell-based therapies and treatments for patients with unmet medical needs.
