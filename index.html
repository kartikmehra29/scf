About Us

Get to know us

Established in 2005, Stem Cell Facility has been a pioneer in the field of Stem cell research. The facility’s main motive is to decipher/explore the potential of stem cells in basic and translational research, thereby, accelerating/pushing forward stem cell-based therapies and treatments for patients with unmet medical needs. Our endeavour is to forge ahead in the ever-evolving field of stem cells, combining regenerative medicine, Tissue engineering & nano Technology. Facility has successfully taken three kinds of stem cells from bench to bedside, namely, Limbal stem cells and oral mucosal stem cells for ocular surface reconstruction; and hair follicle derived stem cells for treating vitiligo. The Facility believes in sharing the knowledge of this ingenuous field among the budding scientific talent, thereby imparting comprehensive training to post graduates and professionals from different institutions across the globe. Our team consists of students and researchers from various backgrounds having different expertise, all focussed towards translating our research from the lab to clinics for a better future. The facility was considered for Department of Biotechnology-Centre of Excellence (DBT-CoE), Govt. of India grant in 2014, with the aim to strengthen the basic, pre- clinical and clinical research. With team of researchers and students we have successfully established the GOOD MANUFACTURING PRACTICES (GMP) FACILITY, in accordance with ICMR- DBT guidelines (Govt. of India) for Stem Cell Research, to manufacture stem cells of clinical grade for patient use. Again in 2017, the DBT-CoESCR-Phase II was sanctioned further promoting the therapeutic applications of stem cells.

Treatment & Support

Dr Sujata Mohanty

After my Post- doctoral research, I joined AIIMS as Scientist-II and later became as a Assistant Professor in 2005. I have been instrumental in setting up this Stem Cell Facility at AIIMS. After taking the charge of the facility in 2005, I initiated basic research using adult and embryonic stem cells. Adult stem cell sources include bone marrow, umbilical cord, adipose tissue, dental pulp, hair and skin follicles. For better understanding of Stem cell biology and drug discovery, iPSC research was initiated at AIIMS. Currently, I am the Professor and Faculty-in-charge of this facility.
